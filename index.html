OUR
STORY

Who We Are

BORN FROM
THE STREET

8K+ Happy Customers
150+ Unique Pieces
4.9 Avg. Rating
40+ Countries Shipped

Noir Collective started in a small studio apartment in 2020, with nothing but a sewing machine, a clear vision, and an obsession with getting every detail right.

We were tired of streetwear that sacrificed quality for hype, and luxury fashion that forgot its roots on the street. So we built something in between — premium garments with authentic street DNA.

"We don't chase trends. We set the tone for people who've never needed permission to be themselves."

Every thread, every stitch, every graphic is deliberately chosen. We work with a small network of sustainable mills and keep our production runs limited — meaning you'll never see half the city wearing the same thing you are.

From our first 50-piece drop that sold out in 6 hours, to shipping globally to over 8,000 customers across 40 countries — we've stayed true to the founding principle: quality over quantity, always.


What Drives Us

OUR VALUES

01
Quality Without Compromise

We use only 350gsm heavyweight cotton, GOTS certified organic where possible. Every garment goes through a 12-point quality check before it leaves our hands.

02
Authentic by Design

No trend-chasing. Our design process starts with culture — music, art, architecture, the streets — and ends with a garment that feels honest and intentional.

03
Planet-Conscious

Limited production runs mean zero excess inventory. We're working toward carbon-neutral shipping by 2026 and reducing our water footprint every season.
